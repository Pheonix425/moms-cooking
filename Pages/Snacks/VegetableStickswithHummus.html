Vegetable Sticks with Hummus

Vegetable sticks with hummus are such a fresh and colorful snack, sweetheart! It’s a great way to get your veggies in while enjoying a creamy, flavorful dip like hummus. The crunch of the vegetables paired with the smooth hummus is just so satisfying. Let’s get you started with this easy, wholesome recipe!
Ingredients
  • 1 cucumber

  • 1 carrot

  • 1 bell pepper (red, yellow, or green)

  • 1 small bunch of celery

  • ½ cup hummus (store-bought or homemade)

  • A drizzle of olive oil

  • A sprinkle of paprika or sesame seeds
Instruction


  1. Preparing the Vegetables - Wash all your vegetables thoroughly. Then, cut the cucumber, carrot, bell pepper, and celery into long, thin sticks, perfect for dipping.


  2. Arranging the Vegetables - Place your vegetable sticks on a plate, arranging them neatly or mixing them together, however you like!


  3. Serving the Hummus - Scoop the hummus into a small bowl, and if you want to add a little extra flavor, drizzle a bit of olive oil on top and sprinkle with paprika or sesame seeds.


  4. Enjoying the Dip - Now dip those fresh vegetable sticks into the hummus and enjoy each bite—it's healthy, refreshing, and delicious!


  5. Storing Leftovers - If you have any leftover veggies, store them in an airtight container with a little water to keep them crisp. Hummus can be refrigerated for a few days in a sealed container.

Extra Knowledge
Vegetable sticks with hummus is a light and nutritious snack, pairing crisp veggies like carrots, cucumbers, and bell peppers with creamy, flavorful hummus. Hummus, a Middle Eastern dip made from blended chickpeas, tahini, lemon, and garlic, provides a rich and savory complement to the fresh crunch of vegetables. This simple combination offers a refreshing way to enjoy plant-based ingredients.hummus is a great source of plant-based protein. It’s also high in fiber, which helps with digestion and keeps you feeling full. Paired with crunchy, fresh vegetables, this snack is not only delicious but packed with nutrients that make your body feel great!
Benifit and disadvantages

Vegetable sticks are packed with vitamins, fiber, and antioxidants, making them a great choice for keeping you healthy and energized. Hummus is rich in protein and healthy fats from chickpeas and olive oil, giving you long-lasting energy. Just remember, hummus can be high in calories, so enjoy it in moderation to keep things balanced!
