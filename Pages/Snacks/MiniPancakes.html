Mini Pancakes

Mini pancakes are such a fun and sweet treat, my dear! They’re perfect for breakfast, a snack, or even dessert. These tiny pancakes are light and fluffy, and you can top them with anything you like—fruit, syrup, or even a little chocolate. Let’s make these together and enjoy a stack of joy!
Ingredients
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour

  • 1 tablespoon sugar

  • 1 teaspoon baking powder

  • 1 pinch of salt

  • ¾ cup milk

  • 1 egg
  • 1 tablespoon melted butter

  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

  • Butter or oil for cooking

  • Maple syrup

  • Powdered sugar

  • Fresh berries
Instruction


  1. Mixing the Dry Ingredients - In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt to combine.


  2. Preparing the Wet Ingredients - In another bowl, mix the milk, egg, melted butter, and vanilla extract until smooth.


  3. Combining the Mixtures - Slowly pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients, stirring gently until you have a smooth batter. Be careful not to overmix!


  4. Cooking the Pancakes - Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat and lightly grease with butter or oil. Drop small spoonfuls of batter onto the pan to form mini pancakes. Cook for 1-2 minutes on each side, or until bubbles form and the edges look set.


  5. Serving - Serve your mini pancakes warm, stacked high on a plate. Add your favorite toppings—whether it’s a drizzle of maple syrup, fresh fruit, or a sprinkle of powdered sugar!

Extra Knowledge
Mini pancakes are bite-sized versions of traditional pancakes, made by pouring small dollops of batter onto a hot griddle. These tiny treats are often served in stacks and topped with butter, syrup, or fresh fruit for a fun and delicious breakfast. They originated from various pancake traditions but have gained popularity for their cute size and easy-to-eat appeal.Perfect for breakfast or a sweet snack, mini pancakes bring a playful twist to the classic—enjoy each fluffy bite!
Benifit and disadvantages

These mini pancakes are made with simple ingredients, offering a nice balance of carbohydrates, proteins, and fats to give you energy. However, they can be high in sugar or calories if topped with too much syrup or sugary toppings, so enjoy them mindfully!
