Yogurt Parfait

Yogurt parfait is such a wonderful treat, my dear! It’s a beautiful blend of creamy yogurt, sweet fruits, and crunchy granola that feels like a little bit of heaven in every spoonful. Not only is it tasty, but it’s also packed with nutrition to help you start your day right or give you an afternoon boost. Let’s make one together and enjoy this lovely, healthy snack!
Ingredients
  • 1 cup plain Greek yogurt (or any yogurt you prefer)

  • ½ cup granola

  • ½ cup mixed berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries)

  • 1 tablespoon honey (optional, for extra sweetness)

  • A few mint leaves for garnish (optional)
Instruction


  1. Layering the Yogurt - Spoon about half of the yogurt into the bottom of a glass or bowl, creating a nice base for your parfait.


  2. Adding the Fruit - Add a layer of mixed berries on top of the yogurt, spreading them out evenly so you get a little bit of fruit in every bite!


  3. Sprinkling the Granola - Next, sprinkle half of the granola over the berries, adding a nice crunch to your parfait.


  4. Repeating the Layers - Repeat the layers by adding the remaining yogurt, followed by more berries and granola on top. Drizzle the honey over the top if you want it a little sweeter.


  5. Garnishing - For a fresh finish, you can garnish your parfait with a few mint leaves. Now, grab a spoon and enjoy every creamy, fruity, crunchy bite!


Extra Knowledge
A yogurt parfait is traditionally made by layering yogurt, fresh fruit, and granola, creating a visually appealing and nutritious dish. The origins of yogurt date back to ancient cultures, where it was valued for its probiotic qualities, though parfaits as we know them became popular in the 19th century. Modern variations often include added toppings like nuts, seeds, or sweet sauces, offering endless possibilities to customize this versatile treat.Whether made with traditional yogurt or dairy-free alternatives, yogurt parfaits offer a delicious way to enjoy fresh ingredients. With endless combinations to try, each parfait brings its own charm—so dig in and enjoy every bite!
