Baked Lemon Garlic Salmon

Baked lemon garlic salmon is such a lovely and healthy dish, sweetheart! It’s not only packed with protein and omega-3 fatty acids but also bursting with flavors that will make your taste buds dance. This dish is simple to prepare and perfect for a nutritious dinner that the whole family will enjoy. Let’s create this delicious meal together!
Ingredients
  • 2 salmon fillets (about 6 oz each)

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

  • 2 cloves garlic, minced

  • Juice of 1 lemon

  • Zest of 1 lemon

  • Salt and pepper (to taste)

  • Fresh parsley or dill (for garnish)
Instruction


  1. Prepping the Oven - Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). This will ensure your salmon cooks evenly and becomes perfectly flaky!


  2. Marinating the Salmon - In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, minced garlic, lemon juice, lemon zest, salt, and pepper. Place the salmon fillets in a baking dish and pour the marinade over them, making sure they are well-coated. Let them sit for about 15-20 minutes to soak up the flavors.


  3. Baking the Salmon - Once marinated, place the baking dish in the preheated oven. Bake the salmon for about 12-15 minutes, or until the fish flakes easily with a fork. Cooking time may vary depending on the thickness of the fillets.


  4. Serving - Once done, carefully remove the salmon from the oven. Serve it warm, garnished with fresh parsley or dill for a lovely touch!


  5. Pairing - This baked salmon pairs wonderfully with steamed vegetables or a fresh salad for a well-rounded meal. Enjoy every bite, my dear!


Extra Knowledge
Did you know that salmon is not only delicious but also packed with nutrients? It’s rich in B vitamins, which help support energy levels, and selenium, an important mineral for thyroid function. Plus, the omega-3 fatty acids found in salmon are known for their anti-inflammatory properties, making it a fantastic choice for maintaining overall health. So, let’s enjoy this healthy meal together and savor the wonderful flavors it brings!
Benifit and disadvantages

Baked lemon garlic salmon is an excellent source of high-quality protein and omega-3 fatty acids, which are great for heart health, sweetheart. The lemon adds a refreshing flavor while providing vitamin C. However, it’s important to enjoy salmon in moderation, as it can contain mercury, especially larger fish. Balancing your seafood intake with a variety of options ensures you get the benefits without overdoing it!
