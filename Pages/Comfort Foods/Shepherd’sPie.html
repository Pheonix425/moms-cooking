Shepherd’s Pie

Shepherd’s Pie is one of those lovely dishes that feels like a warm hug, sweetheart! With layers of tender meat and veggies topped with creamy mashed potatoes, it’s a complete meal that warms both the body and soul. This dish is great for using up leftovers, and I know you’ll love making it just as much as eating it!
Ingredients
  • 1 pound ground lamb or beef (or a mix of both)

  • 1 onion, finely chopped

  • 2 carrots, diced

  • 1 cup frozen peas

  • 2 cloves garlic, minced

  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste

  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 cup beef broth

  • Salt and pepper to taste

  • 4 cups mashed potatoes (store-bought or homemade)

  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme (optional)

  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)
Instruction


  1. Cook the Meat and Vegetables - In a large skillet, cook the ground meat over medium heat until browned.Add the chopped onion, carrots, and minced garlic, cooking until the vegetables are tender, about 5-7 minutes.


  2. Make the Filling - Stir in the tomato paste, Worcestershire sauce, beef broth, and peas.Season with salt, pepper, and thyme if you like. Simmer for about 10 minutes until the mixture thickens.


  3. Prepare the Mashed Potatoes - If you’re using store-bought mashed potatoes, warm them up according to the package instructions.If you’re making them from scratch, mash the cooked potatoes with butter, milk, salt, and pepper until creamy.


  4. Assemble the Pie - Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).In a baking dish, spread the meat mixture evenly, then carefully layer the mashed potatoes on top, smoothing it out with a spatula.


  5. Bake - Bake in the preheated oven for about 20-25 minutes, or until the top is golden and slightly crispy.You can pop it under the broiler for a couple of minutes for extra color if you like.


  6. Serve - Let it cool for a few minutes before serving. Garnish with fresh parsley for a pop of color!
Shepherd’s Pie traditionally uses lamb, while a similar dish made with beef is often called Cottage Pie? This dish has been a staple in many homes, providing nourishment and comfort for generations. You can even customize it by adding your favorite veggies or spices!
Benifit and disadvantages

Shepherd’s Pie is a wonderful dish that offers a hearty combination of protein, veggies, and comforting mashed potatoes, making it a balanced meal. However, it can be quite rich and filling, so it’s best enjoyed in moderation, especially if you're watching your calorie intake.
