Creamy Tomato Soup

there’s nothing quite like a bowl of creamy tomato soup to warm your heart! It’s rich, velvety, and bursting with flavor, just like the kind we’d enjoy together on a chilly day. Pair it with a grilled cheese sandwich, and you have a comforting meal that feels like home. Let’s make this delicious soup together!
Ingredients
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

  • 1 onion, finely chopped

  • 2 cloves garlic, minced

  • 2 cans (14 ounces each) diced tomatoes (with juices)

  • 1 cup vegetable broth (or chicken broth)

  • Salt and pepper to taste

  • 1 teaspoon dried basil (or fresh if you have it)

  • 1 teaspoon sugar (to balance the acidity)

  • 1 cup heavy cream (or half-and-half for a lighter option)

  • Fresh basil leaves, chopped (for garnish)
Instruction


  1. Sauté the Vegetables - In a large pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat.Add the chopped onion and sauté until it’s soft and translucent, about 5 minutes. Stir in the minced garlic and cook for another minute.


  2. Add the Tomatoes and Broth - Pour in the diced tomatoes with their juices and the vegetable broth.Add sugar, dried basil, salt, and pepper. Stir everything together and let it simmer for about 15-20 minutes to develop the flavors.


  3. Blend the Soup - Remove the pot from heat and carefully blend the soup until smooth using an immersion blender. If you don’t have one, you can carefully transfer it to a regular blender in batches.


  4. Make It Creamy - Return the blended soup to the pot over low heat.Stir in the heavy cream until well combined and heated through. Taste and adjust seasoning if needed.


  5. Serve - Ladle the warm soup into bowls and garnish with fresh basil.Serve with crusty bread or a classic grilled cheese sandwich for the perfect combo!


Extra Knowledge
Did you know that tomato soup has been a beloved dish for many generations? It’s often associated with comfort and warmth, especially during cold weather. You can also customize it by adding a pinch of red pepper flakes for a little heat or mixing in some cooked pasta for extra heartiness!
Benifit and disadvantages

Creamy tomato soup is rich in vitamins A and C from the tomatoes, making it a nutritious choice that can support your immune system. However, it can be high in calories and fat due to the cream, so enjoying it in moderation is best for maintaining a balanced diet.
