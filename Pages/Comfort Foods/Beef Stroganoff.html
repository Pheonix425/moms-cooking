Beef Stroganoff

Beef Stroganoff is a classic dish that warms the heart, sweetheart! With tender beef cooked in a creamy mushroom sauce and served over egg noodles, it’s a meal that brings a sense of home and comfort. Perfect for cozy nights, let’s make this delightful dish together!
Ingredients
  • 1 pound beef sirloin, thinly sliced

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

  • 1 onion, finely chopped

  • 2 cups mushrooms, sliced (cremini or button)

  • 2 cloves garlic, minced

  • 1 cup sour cream

  • Salt and pepper to taste

  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour

  • 1 cup beef broth

  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce

  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

  • 8 ounces egg noodles (for serving)

Instruction


  1. Cook the Noodles - Cook the egg noodles according to the package instructions, drain, and set aside.


  2. Sauté the Beef - In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat.Add the sliced beef, season with salt and pepper, and cook until browned. Remove the beef from the skillet and set aside.


  3. Cook the Vegetables - In the same skillet, add the chopped onion and sliced mushrooms.Sauté until the onion is translucent and the mushrooms are tender, about 5-7 minutes. Add the minced garlic and cook for another minute.


  4. Make the Sauce - Sprinkle the flour over the vegetable mixture and stir to combine.Gradually add the beef broth, Worcestershire sauce, and Dijon mustard, stirring until the sauce thickens, about 3-5 minutes.


  5. Combine Everything - Lower the heat and stir in the sour cream until the sauce is creamy.Return the cooked beef to the skillet and heat through. Adjust seasoning with salt and pepper if needed.


  6. Serving - Serve the beef stroganoff over the cooked egg noodles and garnish with fresh parsley.

Extra Knowledge
Did you know that Beef Stroganoff originated in Russia in the 19th century? It has become popular worldwide and is loved for its creamy texture and savory flavor. You can also customize it with different types of mushrooms or even add some peas for extra color and nutrition!
Benifit and disadvantages

Beef Stroganoff is a comforting dish that provides protein and iron from the beef, along with vitamins from the mushrooms. However, it can be rich and high in calories, so enjoying it occasionally and in moderation is the key to a balanced diet.
