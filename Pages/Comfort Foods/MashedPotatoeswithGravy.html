Mashed Potatoes with Gravy

there’s nothing quite like a big scoop of fluffy mashed potatoes topped with rich, savory gravy! This classic dish is a true comfort food that brings warmth to any dinner table. Whether it’s a special occasion or just a cozy night in, this recipe will remind you of the love and care that goes into every bite. Let’s get started on making this delightful dish together!
Ingredients
  • 2 pounds russet or Yukon Gold potatoes, peeled and cut into chunks

  • 1/2 cup milk (or cream for extra richness)

  • 1/4 cup butter, softened

  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup butter

  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour

  • 2 cups beef or chicken broth (low-sodium)

  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • Optional: 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce for extra flavor

Instruction


  1. Cook the Potatoes - Place the peeled and cut potatoes in a large pot and cover them with cold water.Add a pinch of salt and bring to a boil. Cook until the potatoes are tender, about 15-20 minutes. Drain and return them to the pot.


  2. Mash the Potatoes - Add the softened butter and milk to the drained potatoes.Mash them until creamy and smooth. Season with salt and pepper to taste, adjusting to your preference.


  3. Make the Gravy - In a medium saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat.Whisk in the flour, stirring constantly for about 1-2 minutes to form a roux. It should be a light golden color.


  4. Add the Broth - Gradually whisk in the beef or chicken broth, stirring constantly to avoid lumps.Bring the mixture to a simmer and cook until thickened, about 5-7 minutes. Season with salt, pepper, and Worcestershire sauce if using.


  5. Serve - Spoon the creamy mashed potatoes onto plates and generously drizzle with warm gravy.Enjoy with your favorite protein or vegetables!


Extra Knowledge
Did you know that mashed potatoes are a staple in many cultures and can be found in various forms worldwide? They are versatile and can be flavored with garlic, cheese, or herbs for a delicious twist. Remember, making mashed potatoes from scratch always brings a bit of extra love to the table!
Benifit and disadvantages

Mashed potatoes are a great source of carbohydrates and provide energy, while the gravy adds flavor and richness to the dish. However, they can be high in calories and fat, especially with added butter and cream, so it’s best to enjoy them in moderation as part of a balanced meal.
