Macaroni and Cheese

Macaroni and cheese is a classic comfort food that brings joy to the table, sweetheart! With its creamy cheese sauce enveloping tender pasta, it’s like a warm hug in a bowl. You can enjoy it as a side dish or make it a meal on its own. Let’s whip up this delightful dish together!
Ingredients
  • 2 cups elbow macaroni

  • 2 tablespoons butter

  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour

  • 2 cups milk (preferably whole milk)

  • 2 cups shredded sharp cheddar cheese

  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder

  • 1 teaspoon onion powder

  • Salt and pepper to taste

  • Optional toppings: breadcrumbs, paprika, or extra cheese for baking
Instruction


  1. Cook the Pasta - In a large pot of boiling salted water, cook the elbow macaroni according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.


  2. Make the Cheese Sauce - In the same pot, melt the butter over medium heat.Stir in the flour and cook for about 1 minute until it forms a paste (roux).Gradually whisk in the milk, stirring continuously until the mixture thickens, about 3-5 minutes.


  3. Add Cheese and Seasoning - Lower the heat and add the shredded cheddar cheese, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper.Stir until the cheese is completely melted and the sauce is smooth.


  4. Combine Pasta and Sauce - Add the cooked macaroni to the cheese sauce, stirring gently to coat all the pasta evenly with the creamy sauce.


  5. Optional Baking - If you prefer a baked version, preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Transfer the macaroni and cheese to a greased baking dish, sprinkle with breadcrumbs and extra cheese if desired, and bake for 20-25 minutes until golden and bubbly.


Extra Knowledge
Did you know that macaroni and cheese has been a favorite comfort food for centuries? It’s loved for its rich, cheesy flavor and can be customized with different cheeses or add-ins like bacon or vegetables. Each bite is a reminder of home!
Benifit and disadvantages

Macaroni and cheese is a comforting source of carbohydrates and calcium from the cheese, giving you energy and a happy feeling. However, it can be high in calories and saturated fat, so enjoying it in moderation is key to a balanced diet.
